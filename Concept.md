# Modes

## Normal :
Used for moments
I - Insert at the begining of line
i - Insert at one char before the current char.
a - Append at the cursor point.
A - Append at the end of line.
o - start a new line.
w - move  by word (points to begining of other word.)
e - moves by word end (points to end of other word.)
b - move by word backword
$ - Move to the end of the line
0 - Move to the begining of the line
f - Find a specific char ( ; used to move the next occurance.  , used to move to previous occurance)
gg - moves to the begining of file
G  - moves to END of File.
M - moves to visually middle of editor
H - cursor line top
zz - brings the cursor pointing line to center of our visuvalization
% - Used to move to matching brace. (used in programing. cloising to opening viceversa.)


dw - Delete a word (cursor poinitng word copied.)
yw - Copy the entire word
dd - delete the entire linea

p - paste the word after the cursor
P - paste before the cursor
u - undo
ctrl + R - Redo
